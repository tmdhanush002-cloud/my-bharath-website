Textile Machine Monitoring

Vision & Mission

To become India’s leading intelligent textile mill monitoring platform that transforms traditional factories into smart, predictive, and energy-efficient production units.

  • Reduce unexpected breakdowns by 60%
  • Improve yarn quality using real-time analytics
  • Reduce energy waste by 20–30%
  • Enable predictive maintenance

  • SMS alerts to maintenance team
